About Us

Our mission is to act as a catalyst for universal adoption and blockchain innovation. We focus only on investing in cryptocurrency . Our team has experience in both traditional financing and emerging mining technology.

We invest, among others, in cryptocurrencies such as BTC, XRP, ETC, and ETH.

We also give a chance to new, dynamically developing ICO projects that bring huge profits in an amazingly short time.

Our team consists only of qualified people connected with the financial industry for years, who are also passionate about the fledgling but very strong cryptocurrency market and mining technology.

They constantly monitor the market to provide you (our investors) with even greater profits.

Having strong relationships with the most promising entrepreneurs and other leading investors in the industry, Superior Traders implements an investment strategy, building a diversified portfolio and adjusting the added value to its portfolio companies.
